About

     Gamer's Server was born on 2 October 2017 (Monday) at 9:25 am. But before that, it was just a little Steam group called '♡BikiniBottom'. Everything starts up small right? We created this group just to show off the coolness of us while playing Counter Strike Global Offensive, which was the main game we used to play. Everyone had a specified character name of the cartoon 'SpongeBob Squarepants' like Patrick, Garry, Spongebob, Mr. Krab, etc. Sounds funny huh? Besides skills, good teamwork and communication are also required to help us succeed. So, rather than pressing 'k' (mic key) all day long, we found out an easier way to communicate -- Discord.

    Our server was created by me, the frisky admin. It was first named as 'Bikini Bottom' just like the group I've mentioned above. The server join link has never been changed till now because it's better to have a constant invitation link for a long-lasting community. In the beginning, I was just thinking about chatting with my friend with Discord, I didn't expect people would join my server. "gobroman" and "guardian toy" are the two earliest members who showed me huge support in the early stage of raising the server. By the way, they're now retired moderators.

    Not long after Fortnite had been released, we got addicted to it. We would even strike overnight just for one single "chicken dinner".

    Within a few weeks, our server had been pumped with 20+ members during the school holiday in November till December. Thanks to my lovingly friends. During late December in 2017, admin and his friends discussed about the changes that should be made to the server and all of them agreed to rename the server -- Gamers' Server (it was my idea). After that, I continued to create some roles with cute names and promoted some trustworthy buddies as server's moderators.

    In the mid year of 2018, playing Minecraft had become a new trend in our server. No one knows why. There were up to 10 daily players in our minecraft server 'gobroman.aternos.me' (created by "gobroman") back then. By the way, I was the most active player. I would wake up early in the morning and call my friend "gobroman" to re-open the minecraft server. I was totally addicted and mastered a lot of surviving and weapono-enchanting skills. Until today, Minecraft is still one of the hottest games in our server because the game deserves our time.

    In 2019, due to the crappy update of CSGO which attracted a ton of hackers, we decided to play Dota 2 and ended up regarding Dota 2 as our main game until today.

    Time flies, we have already hit 200 members on 25 May 2019. The admin team has been paying a lot of hard work to make this server better. We hope that you'd kindly message us to submit your suggestions and strengthen this big family together.

    If you finished reading all these, I appreciated you for spending valuable time on this passage. I'll update the passage again once we've reached 300 members.
